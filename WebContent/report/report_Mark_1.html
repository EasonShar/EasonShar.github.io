The woman in this photograph has been identified as both Hilda Urlin Petrie and Amy Urlin. The two women were sisters - Hilda Petrie was married to Flinders Petrie, while Amy Urlin was unmarried. The Urlin family was relatively wealthy, and had homes in Sussex and London. Amy was born in 1865, Hilda in 1871. Like many unmarried Victorian ladies, Amy Urlin kept busy helping the less fortunate. She was part of the Charity Organisation Society, a Victorian institution devoted to addressing poverty and family problems.
